<p><a name="S1"></a></p><i class="sc-680f2911-0 hfmHlI"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">/Enters into force on: 2026-01-01/</font></font></i><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  
Information</font></font><p><a name="S2"></a></p><a name="P1"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 1</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The Agency for Youth and Civil Society Affairs is the administrative authority for issues relating to</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. civil society policy,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. youth policy, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3. religious communities.</font></font><p><a name="P1S2"></a></p><a name="P2"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 2</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The authority shall, in its activities pursuant to Section 1,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. produce, collect and disseminate knowledge,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. contribute to the coordination of government efforts,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3. be proactive and supportive towards authorities, municipalities, regions, civil society organisations and other relevant societal actors, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4. distribute government grants.</font></font><p><a name="P2S2"></a></p><a name="P3"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 3</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The authority shall, in its activities pursuant to Section 1,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. promote equal rights and opportunities for young people regardless of sexual orientation, gender identity or gender expression,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. integrate a gender equality perspective, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3. combat discrimination based on gender, transgender identity or expression, ethnicity, religion or other belief, disability, sexual orientation or age.</font></font><p><a name="P3S2"></a></p><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">

In the authority's reports of statistics, follow-ups and analyses, gender shall be an overall basis for classification unless there are specific reasons against this. If relevant, age and other classification bases shall also be used.</font></font><p><a name="P3S3"></a></p><h4 name="Särskilda uppgifter när det gäller genomförandet av politiken  för det civila samhället" class="sc-680f2911-0 eDknKb"><a name="Särskilda uppgifter när det gäller genomförandet av politiken  för det civila samhället"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Specific tasks regarding the implementation of civil society policy</font></font></a></h4><p><a name="P3S4"></a></p><a name="P4"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 4</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The authority shall work to increase knowledge about civil society among public actors and promote collaboration between government agencies and civil society.</font></font><p><a name="P4S2"></a></p><a name="P5"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 5</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The authority shall monitor and analyse the efforts of public actors in relation to the objective of the policy area. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. based on young people's different circumstances and living conditions</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;a) contribute to young people establishing themselves in working and social life,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;b) work to ensure that young people have meaningful and developing leisure activities, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;c) promote young people's opportunities to influence and participate in social development,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. work to ensure that the youth perspective is integrated into the activities of state authorities and support the development of knowledge-based youth policy in municipalities and regions, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3. follow up and analyse young people's living conditions, attitudes and values, at both local and national levels.</font></font><p><a name="P6S2"></a></p><h4 name="Särskilda uppgifter när det gäller trossamfund " class="sc-680f2911-0 eDknKb"><a name="Särskilda uppgifter när det gäller trossamfund "><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Special information regarding religious communities</font></font></a></h4><p><a name="P6S3"></a></p><a name="P7"><b class="sc-680f2911-0 bwRsrO"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">Section 7</font></font></b></a><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">  &nbsp;&nbsp;The authority shall</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2. work to increase knowledge about religion and religious communities among public actors,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3. be responsible for coordinating religious communities in matters relating to crisis preparedness and total defense, and</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4. be responsible for issues regarding religious communities' work with pastoral care.</font></font><p><a name="P7S2"></a></p><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">

The responsibility according to the first paragraph 3 includes that the authority shall </font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. work to ensure that religious communities actively participate in society's crisis preparedness and civil defense, and </font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1. national programme office for Erasmus+ Youth and Erasmus+ Sport,</font></font><br><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font dir="auto" style="vertical-align: inherit;">
